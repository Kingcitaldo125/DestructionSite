Programmers:

Each programmer is a member of the senior class for the Digital Simulation and Game Engineering Technology major at Shawnee State University.

These students have taken classes such as calculus-based physics, data structures,

algorithms, compiler design, computer graphics, networking and communications and artificial intelligence.

Programmers in the Digital Simulation and Game Engineering Technology major also take math and art classes to complement their major.

Artists:

The artists are members of the senior class for the Game and Simulation Development Arts major at Shawnee State University.

The artists also have exposure to programming, to make them more flexible with art and simulation.
