A game about destroying buildings, fighting with other players and having fun. Created with Unreal Engine 4.


ABOUT:

Project Destruction is a 3D 1st person multiplayer survival combat game.

The project is being developed by the senior class of 2017 at Shawnee State University.

Up to 8 players will spawn into an abandoned procedurally generated city with the goal of collecting weapons and utility items to defeat other players.

The objective of each player is to be the last man/woman standing by using both the environment and weapons to their advantage.

The players have a time limit they must abide by, and must fight towards the center of the city as the outer edges of the city are destroyed at set intervals.



GAMEPLAY:

Building Destruction Demo

The center point of the interest in the gameplay is the interactive and destructible nature of the city,

along with the unique weapons that further these interactions. The game will forgo the weaponry of traditional shooters, instead focusing on more exotic weaponry and melee combat.

The players will be able to be thrown around from forces from the weapons and devices which will create a fun,

yet somewhat chaotic gameplay that allows the players to find creative ways to kill other and launch them around for increased damage and demolition.
